What Is PHOENIXHACKS

PhoenixHacks is a non-profit Hackathon hosted by students at Jean Augustine Secondary School. Our goal is to promote interest in Computer Science and STEM related fields. This year we’re inviting Peel high school students to come together and design creative solutions to real world problems.

Our Vision

As a student-led organization, our vision is to empower youth, through our values of Social Innovation and Leadership, by giving them experience in STEM related fields. With these opportunities and values, we hope that PhoenixHacks will be a starting point for the new generation of problem solvers and world leaders.

Why PHOENIXHACKS

Joining PhoenixHacks will not only give participants the opportunity to showcase their talents and skills through our design challenges, but build leadership and long lasting friendships. Additionally, we will be giving out cash-prizes and certificates awards to top finishing teams of the competition. And don’t worry if you don’t finish at the top, we value everyone’s hard work and everyone will receive a participation award.

Frequently Asked Questions

    What is a Hackathon?

    A hackathon is a community-based event where people from interested in STEM come together to design unique solutions to current day issues.

    Who Can Attend?

    All Peel District School board students from Grades 9-12 can participate.

    What Do I Need to Participate at Home?

    A reliable internet connection and a computer.

    How Do I Register for the Competition?

    Registration will open April 1st, 2021. Be sure to register and get updates.

    What if I don’t know how to Code ?

    PhoenixHacks is a beginner-friendly hackathon, and doesn’t require any experience from our participants. Just join with an open-mind and be ready to take on a challenge.

    Will I win any prizes?

    Definitely! Even though meeting new people and acquiring new technical skills are important, top teams that show the most passion and dedication to their design will win various cash prizes and will receive certificates.
